CMS Website Design

A content management system (CMS) is an application that is used to manage website content, allowing multiple contributors to create, edit and publish. Content in a CMS are stored in a database and displayed in a presentation layer based on a set of templates.

TAS has developed an in-house Content Management System, to best serve our clients. Our team built a flexible system that is able to host any bespoke designs, content and custom application extensions.

Best of all, our CMS is responsive to the device displaying it so that it looks great no matter the size of your device screen.

Benefits of CMS

  • Customised and tailored for your business needs
  • Highly flexible
  • Easily add, edit, and delete content
  • Change appearance of the site
  • Updates are instantly reflected on your site
  • Context sensitive and responsive to screen size
  • Hosts designs, content, custom application extensions
